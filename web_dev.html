Web Development

I have formal web development training from Saskatchewan Polytechnic (Diploma in Computer Systems Technology) and have experience building and maintaining church and other websites. I strive to understand the unique needs of each church. Through my studies in theology (Bachelor of Theology in Church Ministry from Ambrose University) and my experience serving in volunteer and paid positions in several very different churches, I have gained an appreciation and understanding of what congregants and newcomers look for on church websites. I am particularly interested in creating a faithful online representation of the church for newcomers who may be checking out the church website as a first step as they consider attending a Sunday service.

Whether you need a complete redesign or help maintaining your current website, I am excited to partner with you.
