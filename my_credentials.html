About Me

I’m Brandon, a web developer and audio visual (AV) technician with a passion for helping churches navigate the online and AV spaces in a way that enhances their existing ministries. My first goal is always to understand what your church wants and needs. From there, I’ll collaborate with you to determine the best path forward.

My background in ministry, tech, and theology has uniquely equipped me for this work. My qualifications include a Bachelor or Theology in Church Ministry from Ambrose University, a Diploma in Computer Systems Technology from Saskatchewan Polytechnic, and a variety of ministry roles at several different churches. My ministry experience ranges from traditional ministry roles with kids and youth to tech-oriented roles.
